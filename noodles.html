Indomie

Decription

This recipe is used to make indomie-noodles that even beginners can follow

Ingredients

  • indomie-noodles
  • pepper
  • tomatoes
  • onions
  • water
  • eggs
  1. Put the pot on fire and add water
  2. When the water has started boiling you put in the indomie-noodlesinto the pot and allow to boil
  3. Add the spices that came along with the noodles along your tomatoe, onion, pepper and any other spice that you wish to add
  4. Cover the pot with the lid and allow the water to dry
  5. Put it in a plate and enjoy.
